# Withdrawing Earnings

Make sure to connect your Web3 wallet with the WebApp. The address should be the same you used to register your operator with.

1. Browse to the [My Account page](https://app.ssv.network/operators) and **select the Operator you want to withdraw from**.

![withdraw](/img/withdraw-earnings-1.png)

2. **Click on the _Withdraw_ button** at the bottom side of the screen.

![withdraw](/img/withdraw-earnings-2.png)

3. **Click on the _Withdraw all_ button**.

<div style={{ display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center' }}>
  <img 
    src="/img/withdraw-earnings-3.png" 
    alt="Withdraw earnings" 
    style={{ width: '60%', maxWidth: '600px' }}
  />
</div>

4. **Sign the transaction**.

A smart contract transaction is generated by the WebApp, make sure to open your Web3 wallet, if it does not automatically and confirm the transaction.

<div style={{ display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center' }}>
  <img 
    src="/img/withdraw-earnings-4.png" 
    alt="Withdraw earnings" 
    style={{ width: '45%', maxWidth: '500px' }}
  />
</div>

5. **Done!**

The WebApp will update, waiting for the transaction to be confirmed by the network, when this happens, the funds will have been transferred to the Operator owner wallet.
