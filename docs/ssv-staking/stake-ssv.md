# Stake SSV

Stake SSV to receive cSSV, start earning rewards, and automatically delegate the voting power tied to your staked weight to the SSV Network oracle system.

## Prerequisites

1. SSV tokens in your wallet
2. A compatible Web3 wallet or wallet connection method (for example, MetaMask or WalletConnect)
3. Sufficient ETH for gas fees
4. Access to the [SSV Network Web App](https://app.ssv.network/)

## Staking Process

#### 1. Connect Your Wallet

Open the [SSV Network Web App](https://app.ssv.network/), select **Connect Wallet**, and approve the connection.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-1.png)

#### 2. Navigate to Staking

Open the **Stake** section to begin staking.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-2.png)

#### 3. Enter Staking Amount

Select **Stake SSV**, enter the amount stake, and review the details.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-3.png)

#### 4. Approve SSV Token *(First Time Only)*

If this is your first time staking, approve the staking contract to access your SSV tokens. Select **Approve SSV** and confirm the transaction in your wallet. You only need to do this once per wallet.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-4.png)

#### 5. Confirm Staking Transaction

Select **Stake**, review the transaction in your wallet, and confirm it. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed onchain in the Web App.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-5.png)

#### 6. Receive cSSV

After confirmation, you receive cSSV in your wallet and rewards start accruing immediately. The updated staking position appears in the dashboard under **Staked Balance**.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-6.png)
