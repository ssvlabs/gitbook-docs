# Stake SSV

## Prerequisites

1. SSV tokens in your wallet
2. Compatible Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
3. Sufficient ETH for gas fees
4. Access to the [SSV Network Web App](https://app.ssv.network/)

## Staking Process

#### 1. Connect Your Wallet

Navigate to the [SSV Network Web App](https://app.ssv.network/), click "Connect Wallet", and approve the connection.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-1.png)

#### 2. Navigate to Staking

Go to the "Stake" section to start the staking process.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-2.png)

#### 3. Enter Staking Amount

Click "Stake SSV", enter the amount you wish to stake, and review the details.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-3.png)

#### 4. Approve SSV Token *(First Time Only)*

If this is your first time staking, approve the staking contract to access your SSV tokens. Click "Approve SSV" and confirm the transaction. Note, you only need to do this once.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-4.png)

#### 5. Confirm Staking Transaction

Click "Stake", review the transaction details in your wallet, and confirm. You will see the green checkmark once the transaction is confirmed on-chain.

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-5.png)

#### 6. Receive cSSV

Once confirmed, you'll receive cSSV tokens in your wallet and start earning rewards immediately. The changes will be reflected on the dashboard, note the "Staked Balance".

![Stake SSV](/img/stake-ssv-6.png)
